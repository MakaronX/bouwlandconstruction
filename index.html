FAQ
What is the work schedule for your craftsmen?
We individually draw up a work schedule for each client. Specialists start work at 8.00 or another time convenient for you. Also, if necessary, we can work on holidays and weekends.
In what case can the cost of services increase in the course of work?
The cost of services may change in case you need additional services or when painting you decide to replace white paint with color, since color painting is longer and more difficult. In all other cases, our prices remain the same as at the conclusion of the contract!
How are services paid?
Payment is made in stages for a large amount of work or in one payment (upon completion of work), if the order is small.
Will I be able to discuss the progress of work with specialists at the site?
Yes, there will definitely be people among the masters with knowledge of Dutch, Russian and English, so you will not have any problems communicating with them.
How long will a specialist visit me to take measurements?
The foreman will arrive at the site within 24 hours after the consultation. In rare cases, by prior agreement, a specialist can visit you within 24 - 48 hours.
How much does the visit of the master to the object cost?
A visit of a specialist to assess the volume, cost of work and the choice of materials is absolutely free. Even if the proposed samples of materials or the cost of our services do not suit you, you still do not need to pay for the exit.
