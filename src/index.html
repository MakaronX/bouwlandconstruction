ABOUT US
Bouwland Construction is a construction company that performs turnkey renovations in Amsterdam and the suburbs. We repair homes, commercial buildings and other types of buildings. Attentive foremen and preparation of design projects, taking into account the customer's wishes.
The work is carried out by certified professionals and on time we give a second life to old buildings and modern stylish renovation in new residential complexes and homes. Our craftsmen use equipment and materials from trusted manufacturers.
